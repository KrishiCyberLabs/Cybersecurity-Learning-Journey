# Careers in Cyber

This room introduced the major career paths within cybersecurity and the responsibilities associated with each role. It provided insight into how security teams operate and how different professionals contribute to protecting organizations from cyber threats.

### Skills Gained

* Understanding cybersecurity career domains
* Familiarity with common security roles
* Knowledge of industry terminology
* Awareness of offensive and defensive security functions

### Reflection

One of the biggest lessons from this room was realizing that cybersecurity is much more than penetration testing. The diversity of roles in the industry highlighted multiple career paths and reinforced my motivation to pursue a career in cybersecurity.
